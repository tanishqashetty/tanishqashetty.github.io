GenAI Agent · Solo PM Project · YouTube Studio · Inspiration Tab · React + Claude API

Creative Replay

A GenAI agent inside YouTube Studio that reads your last video's retention curve 48 hours after posting, diagnoses the structural drop-off, and delivers one specific creative brief for your next video. Not a report. One brief, one action, go again. Lives inside the Inspiration tab — the Inspiration tab is for discovering new ideas; Creative Replay makes the next idea better-informed by the last one.

Discovery: 10 creator interviews Mar – Apr 2026 V1 prototype shipped Claude API · React · Vite
Research foundation
Research foundation · 10 creator interviews · Mar–Apr 2026
The finding
8 of 10 creators said the same thing
"I can see where I lost them. I don't know what to change." Every creator had the data. None had a workflow to act on it.
How the agent works
48 hrs post-publish → retention curve → one brief
Creator inputs niche, concept, and retention curve in plain language. Claude diagnoses the structural issue and outputs hook format, reveal timing, and re-engagement placement.
Why the name
Review the tape. Change one thing. Go again.
Inspiration tab = discover new ideas. Creative Replay = get better at executing the idea you already had. Creator language, not product language.
Key product decisions
Input design
Retention signal, not the video
Creators told me they didn't want to re-upload footage. The shape of the drop-off is the insight — not the content. In V2, this pulls directly from the Analytics API.
Output format
Brief, not report
Analytics already reports. Three creators said they close the analytics tab feeling vague guilt. Creative Replay closes the session with a next action.
Measurement
One metric per brief
Multiple metrics split attention and make it impossible to attribute a change. Average view duration on the next video is the single north star — did the structural fix work?
Why this belongs in the Inspiration tab
Enhancement, not a new surface
The Inspiration tab already lives where creators go to decide what to make next. Creative Replay adds a "from your last video" layer — making that decision data-informed rather than purely intuition-driven. No new habit required.
